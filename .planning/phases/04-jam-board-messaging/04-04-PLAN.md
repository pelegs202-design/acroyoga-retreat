  - Level badge (colored tag: beginner=green, intermediate=yellow, advanced=red, all=neutral)

  - Attendee avatars: stacked pill — up to 4 overlapping circles (avatar images or initials), then "+N more" text. Use tailwind `-ml-2` for overlap. Each avatar is a small circle (h-6 w-6) with ring-2 ring-neutral-900 for separation.

- Optimistic UI: update button state immediately, revert on error
- Card styling: bg-neutral-900 with neutral-800 border, rounded, p-4. Brutalist dark theme. Brand accent on primary action.
